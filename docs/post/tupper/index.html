Welcoming Andrew Tupper to the lab!

NJ Grünwald
Aug 15, 2020 1 min read

We welcome Dr. Andrew Tupper to our group. Andrew graduated with a PhD degree in Biochemistry and Astrobiology from McMaster University in Hamilton, ON, Canada. He previously received his BS in Interdisciplinary Studies from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, NY. Andrew has published research with intriguing titles such as ‘Can the RNA World Still Function without Cytidine?’

Andrew joins our group to conduct comparative genomic analyses of Phytophthora ramorum and close relatives.
